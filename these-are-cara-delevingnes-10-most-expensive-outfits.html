Making her red carpet debut at the London Fashion Week for Burberry in 2011, Cara Delevingne has become a sought-after figure and a red carpet fixture for many events. Known for her head-turning looks, Cara's fashion transformation has gone from wearing bespoke gowns to donning tailored suits and cut-out dresses. As she entered the world of acting and starred in several mainstream movies and television shows like Carnival Row, Suicide Squad, and Paper Towns, Delevingne has used the press junkets as an opportunity to show her fashion style with the help of her stylist Mariel Haenn.

From wearing outfits of her staple brand Dior to changing the style to Burberry, Cara Delevingne's red carpet looks are gorgeous.

Dior AW18 Fashion Show

Dior’s Fall 2018 Fashion Show was an extravaganza, and many A-list celebrities made their appearances wearing outfits by creative designer Maria Grazie Chiuri. Known for her combination of streetwear aesthetic with elegance, Delevingne was dressed for the front row. As mentioned by Vogue, the starlet wore a glamorous crop top and trousers that were embroidered with the word J’Adore and a long checkered overcoat to complete the look.

Carnival Row Berlin Screening

Delevingne was already in the news for her daring outfits at the Carnival Row premieres, and she enchanted everyone at the Berlin Screening of the movie. Dressed in a liquid-design dress from the David Koma Resort 2020, the nude variations on the gown were sparkling with sheer sequins. Never big on jewelry, she completed her look with black nail polish.

2019 MET Gala

A custom-made Dior for the biggest fan of the fashion house, Cara Delevingne’s outfit was inspired by a circus theme. Made from sheer, it featured a tulle playsuit with colored satin bands in rainbow colors. She wore gold heels, matching tights, and a Machine Dazzle-designed headpiece showcased objects like bananas, mouths, eyeballs, and fried eggs. As reported by Harper’s Bazaar, it took 600 hours to make the outfit.

2014 GQ Men Of The Year

Delevingne went for a daring number with her Burberry barely-there dress made from sheer. Her sophisticated look was completed with a dark statement lipstick, open-toed black boots, and a simple gold chain. She had arrived at the event wearing a teddy bear coat in a mustard shade that matched her sling purse.

2017 MTV Movie And TV Awards

MTV Movie And TV Awards are known for the over-the-top and outlandish outfits celebrities wear. Cara Delevingne brought her A-Game to the red carpet when she put together a gritty ensemble. Dressed in Fall 2017 Saint Laurent minidress, she paired her look with Anita Ko jewelry and velvet boots. She also debuted her new buzz cut, which became the talk of the town.

2017 Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets Premiere Paris

Once again, showing her bold attitude with a statement outfit, Delevingne shimmered at the Paris Premiere of her movie Valerian And The City Of A Thousand Planets. She wore a couture Alexandre Vauthier gown with a thigh-high slit. She completed her bold look with black Louboutin heels and bold red lipstick.

2017 Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets London Premiere

Not a red but a blue carpet for the starlet, Cara Delevingne looked every bit perfect in a custom black tailored suit by Burberry for the London Premiere. According to Elle, her suit was styled with a jewel crystal capelet inspired by vintage British jewelry. Her platinum blonde pixie cut was styled with a sleek black ribbon while she accessorized her suit with a pair of black heels.

2019 Carnival Row LA Premiere

When it came to dominating the red carpet of the LA Premiere of her Prime TV show Carnival Row, Cara Delevingne wore a hypnotic dress that drew all eyes towards her. She was dressed in a laser-cut dress from Iris van Herpen’s collection called Hypnosis which was designed with a trippy texture. She completed her outfit with Stuart Weitzman heels and diamond earrings.

2017 Met Gala

A regular at the Met Gala, Cara Delevingne changes her fashion every year to stay in line with the theme. 2017 had the theme of Rei Kawakubo/Comme des Garçons: Art of the In-Between, and Delevingne was dressed in a Chanel brocade pantsuit from the Spring 2017 collection. Made from sleek material and reflective surfaces, she gave her shaved head metallic paint.

2018 Met Gala

The 2018 Met Gala saw some of the most beautiful looks by celebrities due to its Heavenly Bodies theme. As stated by The Hollywood Reporter, Cara was one of the best-dressed celebrities in her custom Dior all-black ensemble. Her cage gown was created with sheer lattice-patterned panels, and her Dior crown was made from encrusted beads with a beaded veil.

From wearing sheer and lace gowns that show off her daring outfits to wearing style-statement pantsuits that have become a staple in the Cara Delevingne red carpet wardrobe, the starlet is known to wear some of the most glamorous outfits that have made her a fashion icon for the new generation. She was last seen playing a supporting role in the hit Hulu series Only Murders In The Building.
