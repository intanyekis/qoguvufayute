Middletown High School Lockdown: Two Students Injured in Stabbing Incident at Monhagen Middle School

July 2024 Â· 1 minute read

A horrific incident unfolded at Monhagen Middle School in Middletown, where two students were stabbed, leading to a lockdown of the school. According to sources, a student brought a knife to school, which led to the stabbing.

Victims and Injuries

The two victims are currently being treated for their injuries. The severity and nature of their injuries have not yet been disclosed.

Immediate Response

Officials responded swiftly to the scene, securing the area and placing the school on lockdown. Parents were advised not to go to the scene for safety reasons. Nearby Maple Hill Elementary School was also placed on lockdown as a precaution.

Investigation Underway

The circumstances surrounding the incident are currently under investigation. Law enforcement agencies are working diligently to gather all relevant information and evidence.

Impact on School Operations

Following the incident, classes were canceled for the day at Monhagen Middle School. It is unclear when normal operations will resume.

Conclusion

This distressing event has left the community shaken. As we await further updates on the condition of the victims and the ongoing investigation, our thoughts are with the students, staff, and families affected by this tragic incident.
