Who is Asher Angel?

Asher Angel is an American child actor. He is best known for his starring role as Jonah Beck in the TV series Andi Mack (2017-present).

Asher has performed in more than 10 theater productions. He is also known for playing the character Brad Jr. in the film Jolene (2008).

Asher Angel’s Early life, childhood, and education

Asher Angel was born on September 6, 2002, in the United States of America.

His birth name is Asher Dov Angel. His mother’s name is Coco Angel and his father’s name is Jody Angel.

He has two younger siblings named Avi Angel and London Angel. Asher is an American by nationality and he is of English ethnicity.

Asher Angel had an interest in acting since his early childhood age and he has a dream of becoming a professional full-time actor in the future.

Asher Angel’s Career, net worth
