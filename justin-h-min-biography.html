Who is Justin H. Min?

Justin H. Min is a famous American actor and model. He started his career with roles in Wong Fu productions.

He rose to prominence after appearing as Ben Hargreeves in the Netflix show The Umbrella Academy in 2019. Min is a photographer as well as a former journalist who had worked in many magazines.

How many siblings does Justin H. Min have?

Justin H. Min was born in Cerritos, California, the United States on March 20, 1990. His nationality is American and as of 2023, he is 33 years old. He is of Korean-American ethnicity and his birth zodiac sign is Pisces.

He was born to his Korean parents. Though, the actor has not opened up about his parents and siblings to the media. In 2007, he went to Cerritos High School and later graduated from Cornell University.

Then, In 2011, he earned his degree from the school’s College of Arts & Sciences with a major in Government and English.

Justin H. Min- Professional Life, Careers

Before entering acting, Justin H. Min served as a journalist and photographer for different magazines. He served as a photographer in the J.A.M. Awards, The Served, and inVISIBLE.

Min started to appear in acting in 2012. Firstly, he made appearances on TV shows like Faking It, CSI: Cyber, and Pure Genius. He appeared in several roles on Wong Fur Productions. It is a liberal production firm located in Los Angeles, being cast in the short movie How I Became an Adult and their 2019 web shows Dating After College, among other works.

In 2019, he starred as Ben Hargreeves in the Netflix show The Umbrella Academy after which he was offered to act as a lead role in season 2. Min did not participate in the promotional activities of the show not to reveal his character. In the same year, he starred as Colin Farrell in the lead role in the science fiction movie After Yang.

Though the film was rumored to be released in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the release date was delayed to 2021 in the United States.

Likewise, his other movie The Yang is on the way to be released on March 4, 2022, by A24 both on Showtime and in theaters. Kogonada is the writer, director, and editor of the movie while Colin Farrell, Malea Emma Tjandrawidjaja, Haley Lu Richardson, and Jodie Turner-Smith are the co-stars of him. The movie was launched on July 8, 2021, at the Cannes Film Festival.
