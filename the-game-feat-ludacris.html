Overall Meaning

In the song Ya Heard by The Game, he talks about his success and his luxurious lifestyle. He boasts about his fancy cars, his diamond chains, and his love for playfully shooting guns. He also touches on his controversies with other rappers in the industry who he feels are getting more recognition than him. He mentions Kanye West and Lil Wayne and asserts that even though they are widely accepted as two of the best rappers in the game, he deserves more recognition because he has sold 7 million records. Ludacris also features in the song and talks about his wealth, his talent, and his musical superiority. Both rappers encourage their fans to turn up the radio and listen to their music because their hits show that they are back in the game.


One of the interesting facts about this song is that it was one of the most streamed tracks on The Game's album, LAX, in 2008. Another fascinating fact about the song is that the beat was produced by Nottz, who is a renowned producer in the hip-hop industry. On this song, he samples If You Can't Beat 'Em, Join 'Em by Tangerine Dream, and it adds a nice touch to the beat. The song received critical acclaim from many music critics and was regarded as one of the best songs on the album. The music video for the song was launched on MTV Jams, and it got over 3 million views on YouTube. The Game references several other high-profile rappers in the song, including Diddy, Lil Kim, and Jay-Z, showing his influence in the rap industry.


The guitar chords for Ya Heard by The Game are Am, F, and G.
