CJ Perry Reacts To Miros Very Petty Actions

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Former WWE Superstar and wife of former AEW TNT Champion Miro, is now officially All Elite. She recently shared a message for the self-proclaimed God’s Favorite Champion.

Fans had been wondering for a long time when Lana (a.k.a CJ Perry) would join her husband in the Jacksonville-based promotion. At the AEW All Out pay-per-view, she made her presence felt. The former WWE Superstar showed up to help her husband against Powerhouse Will Hobbs.

Ever since showing up, Miro had been running away from his wife. He claimed that he had renounced her. Following that, the Ravishing Russian claimed that while the self-proclaimed God’s Favorite Champion does not want her, she is determined to be on the hunt for championship gold.

“You might have left but my drive for gold did not. #Forsaken,” CJ Perry tweeted.

Stop meddling!

Since then, the hot and flexible wife of Miro has been looking for clients whom she could manage in AEW. She’s been scouting for talent and this past week on Collision, Action Andretti caught up with CJ Perry backstage. He seemed to show interest in acquiring the former WWE Superstar’s managerial services.

This did not sit well with Miro as he attacked Andretti, claiming he was protecting his wife.

CJ Perry was furious following Miro’s actions. She took to Twitter, claiming that the former AEW TNT Champion was being petty. She also asked him to stop hurting her potential clients.

“@ToBeMiro this is very petty. You renounced me ! Stop meddling in the launch of my management company & hurting potential clients out of JEALOUSY!” CJ Perry tweeted.

Miro’s actions have led to a match between him and Andretti next week on Collision.
