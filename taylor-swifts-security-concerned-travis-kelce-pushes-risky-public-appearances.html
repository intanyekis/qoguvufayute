Taylor Swifts Security Is Concerned As Travis Kelce Pushes Her To Take More Risky Public Appearan

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift enjoyed a low-key date at Coachella, where they were seen watching Lana Del Rey together in the crowd.
  • Despite potential security concerns, the couple seemed comfortable and were spotted enjoying the music festival alongside other concert-goers.
  • Travis Kelce's desire for more public outings with Taylor Swift is causing concern among their security team due to safety compromises.

Taylor Swift is arguably one of the most recognizable faces on the planet, so it’s nearly impossible for her to go out without being noticed. If she steps out, she’s often accompanied by security to ensure her safety at all times. But things may be changing with Travis Kelce, as he’s reportedly pushing what her security team feels is comfortable, as evidenced by the couple’s appearance at Coachella.

Travis And Taylor Went Solo In The Crowd At Coachella

Travis and Taylor were in the crowd to watch her friend Lana Del Rey’s headlining performance. The couple kept low-key in casual outfits, with Travis’ arms wrapped tightly around Taylor as the enjoyed the music in the packed crowd.

Other concert goers captured footage of the couple during the cute moment, showing that while Taylor was getting recognized by those around her, she was seemingly being left in peace. It’s possible the couple had security accompanying them in plainclothes. Some social media sleuths are convinced a man beside them made eye contact with the camera, signalling he was watching and, if so, was likely part of Taylor’s security.

However, with the presence of her security being unconfirmed, it’s possible that Travis and Taylor were enjoying the performance just the two of them, which would be a rarity for Taylor given her fame. Even if security is present, the fact that the pop star felt comfortable being in the crowd could signal a new era for the singer in her personal life.

Travis Is Pushing For Taylor To Attend Another Festival With Him

Nevertheless, one report claims Taylor’s security is uncomfortable with how far Travis is willing to go to have “normal” public outings, as it compromises both of their safety. Apparently, Travis is also pushing for Taylor to accompany him to another festival, Neon Lights, and their security is trying to organize with the festival planners to ensure her safety.

"Travis loves Coachella and he was keen to go to Neon, it's the biggest after-party of the weekend," a source told The U.S. Sun. "His team was trying to figure out the logistics for him and Taylor to attend but it has been up in the air due to the attention they get from crowds.

"Despite being an invite-only event there are still thousands of partygoers at the bash every year and Taylor and Travis always think about safety first,” they continued.

Travis and Taylor have had several big security concerns in recent months. The pop star was faced with a stalker who tried getting into her New York condo multiple times, even after being arrested. Moreover, Travis has confessed to having to sell his Kansas home after Swifties discovered his address. He later bought a $6 million mansion with enhanced security features, but even his family has expressed concerns for Travis and Taylor’s safety.

With Taylor’s new album dropping in a few days, her star power will only likely rise and same with the fame associated with her stardom.
