The CoinOut Founder: Jeff Witten

The founder of CoinOut is Jeff Witten, a Columbia Law School graduate with a background in technology and finance. Jeff Witten’s journey towards creating CoinOut began during a trip to a coin exchange kiosk, where he recognized the need for a digital solution to handle loose change. Inspired by this experience and his expertise in technology and finance, Witten set out to bridge the gap between cash transactions and digital payments.

With the goal of providing users with a convenient way to digitize their spare change, Witten developed CoinOut as a platform for storing virtual currency. Prior to its appearance on Shark Tank, CoinOut was already operational and gaining traction in the finance app market, showcasing the visionary mindset and entrepreneurial spirit of its founder.

Jeff Witten’s educational background in law, combined with his passion for technology and finance, played a crucial role in the creation and success of CoinOut. His innovative approach and determination have helped shape the app into a valuable tool for users looking to optimize their financial transactions.

CoinOut on Shark Tank

CoinOut made its much-anticipated appearance on Season 9, Episode 23 of the hit TV show Shark Tank. Founder Jeff Witten took the stage to present his innovative digital receipt tracking platform, demonstrating how users can effortlessly scan their receipts to claim rewards. The episode showcased the potential of CoinOut and garnered significant attention from both the sharks and the audience.

During his pitch, Witten sought a $250,000 investment in exchange for a 7.5% equity stake in the company. The sharks had mixed reactions, with Kevin O’Leary expressing concerns about competition in the cashback app industry. However, Barbara Corcoran and Kevin O’Leary both made competitive offers that caught Witten’s attention.

Barbara Corcoran proposed a $250,000 investment for a 15% equity stake, but with a condition. Corcoran insisted that CoinOut must partner with 700 new retail locations within the next six months to secure the deal. This offer showcased Corcoran’s confidence in the app’s potential and her belief in its ability to scale rapidly.

Although the intriguing offer from Barbara Corcoran piqued Witten’s interest, ultimately, the deal did not proceed beyond the Shark Tank show. However, this setback did not deter CoinOut’s success and growth.
