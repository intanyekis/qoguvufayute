Who is Brooke Langton?

Brooke Langton is an American actress who is mostly known as Samantha Reilly in the Fox prime-time soap opera ‘Melrose Place. She is also a model.

She is also known as Angela Bennett from the television series ‘The Net’.

Brooke has appeared in numerous movies like ‘Shifting Gears’, ‘The Replacement’ and ‘Swingers’.

Brooke Langton: Bio, Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Langton was born in a copper-mining town in Arizona, USA on 27 November 1970.

Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is North American. She is 52 years old.

She was raised in Illinois, Texas, and Japan. Her father Jackson Langton was a geologist and her mother was a surgical nurse.

As a child, Brooke Langton took an interest in acting listening to her grandfather’s stories about actors. She is related to Swedish actor Adolf Jahr.

In which school did Brooke Langton attend?

Langton has studied in Herscher High School and L.D. Bell High School.

Later, she attended San Diego State University for further study majoring in marine biology.

Brooke Langton: Professional Life and Career

Firstly, Langton began her career as a model appearing in several commercials while she was living in Japan.

Then moving back to the United States she started her acting career. During that time she studied at Second City Improv.

In 1992, she made her television debut appearing on several episodes of ‘Beverly Hills, ‘Freshman Dorm’ and ‘Baywatch’.

Then she made a film debut as a drug addict Jump Junkie in the action film ‘Terminal Velocity.

In 1996, Brooke Langton got a recurring role in the television series Melrose Place where she portrayed her role as Samantha Reilly.

Then she was cast as a lead in the USA Network series ‘The Net’.

Since then her career took on a successful path being known to the world.

She has been active in the acting industry since 1992 and is still currently active.
