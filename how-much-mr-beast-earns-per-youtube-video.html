How Much Money Does Mr Beast Earn As A Result Of His YouTube Career?

In an interview with Graham Stephan, Mr Beast explained what he earns from his YouTube videos. While there's no way of knowing what money he makes from each specific video since he has uploaded so many, he earns millions of dollars monthly.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Mr Beast has a $100 million net worth. The publication says that he earns a monthly salary between $3 million and $5 million. Advertisements make up $3 million of his monthly revenue. Sportskeeda reported that for every video that he creates, Jimmy Donaldson could make $500-$5,000.

Donaldson told Stephan to focus on a random channel and see what money he earned. Stephan looked amazed and impressed and mentioned that one channel had made $63,000 in one month.
