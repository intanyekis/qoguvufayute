Josh Altman: A Quick Look at His Biography

Renowned for his role in the reality TV show “Million Dollar Listing” on Bravo TV, Josh Altman has established himself as a prominent figure in the real estate industry in the United States, particularly in The Platinum Triangle, encompassing Bel Air, Holmby Hills, Sunset Strip, and Beverly Hills, where he represents Hilton & Hyland.

As of early 2017, sources estimated Josh Altman’s net worth to be over $10 million, amassed through a career spanning more than 15 years, which includes his real estate ventures and television appearances.

Josh Altman’s Net Worth: $10 Million

Josh Altman, born on March 10, 1979, in Newton, Massachusetts, hails from a Jewish family. He graduated from Newton South High School in 1997 and obtained a degree in Speech Communications from Syracuse University in 2001.

Relocating to LA, Josh began his real estate career by flipping houses and went on to sell over $100 million in real estate while working with his brother at their real estate brokerage, The Altman Brothers.

He gained recognition for sealing major real estate deals, including the notable $16.5 million house sale in Beverly Hills and the sale of the highest-priced condo in Santa Monica for $10.5 million.

Josh’s association with celebrities and his high-value real estate transactions led to him selling over $40 million in real estate in 2011 and exceeding $75 million in sales the following year. He was even ranked 30th by The Wall Street Journal, having completed over $200 million in sales in the past year.

Besides real estate, Josh Altman is also involved in investment activities and serves as a consultant for renowned media outlets. He has made appearances on popular reality shows like “Keeping Up with the Kardashians” and “Giuliana and Bill”, in addition to featuring on “Million Dollar Listing”. In 2015, he authored the book “It’s Your Move”.

Josh Altman is married to Heather Bilyeu, and the couple resides in Los Angeles.
