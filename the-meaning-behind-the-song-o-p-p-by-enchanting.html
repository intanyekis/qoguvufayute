The Meaning Behind The Song: O.P.P. by Enchanting

August 2024 · 3 minute read
TitleO.P.P.
ArtistEnchanting
Writer/ComposerEnchanting, Jdot Breezy & Whoppa Wit Da Choppa
AlbumNo Luv (2022)
Release DateNovember 14, 2022
GenreRap, Freestyle, Texas Rap, Florida Rap, Trap, Gangsta Rap
ProducerBWolf201 & TAPEKID

When it comes to music, sometimes the lyrics can hold a deeper meaning than what meets the ear. One such song is “O.P.P.” by Enchanting. Released on her album “No Luv” in 2022, this rap track has gained popularity for its catchy beats and honest lyrics.

From the very beginning, Enchanting sets the tone with strong, assertive verses. She portrays a sense of confidence and power, making it clear that she won’t tolerate fake individuals. The line “I can’t take niggas for real, they ass be all talk” speaks to her ability to see through people’s facades. Enchanting demands authenticity from those around her.

The song delves into themes of loyalty and love. Enchanting addresses the struggles of relationships and the difficulties of trust. She acknowledges her own shortcomings and admits to occasionally misleading her partner. Despite this, she emphasizes the importance of communication and remains unapologetic for her actions.

Enchanting’s lyrics also touch upon the idea of materialism and the influence it can have on a relationship. She desires a partner who can provide for her, stating, “I need a nigga wit’ the most, can’t settle for no less.” This highlights her determination to live a comfortable life and not settle for anything less than what she feels she deserves.

As an artist, Enchanting uses her platform to express her feelings and experiences. The rawness of her lyrics and the authenticity of her delivery create a connection between herself and her audience. Listeners can relate to the struggles she faces in relationships and the desire for genuine connections.

Personally, “O.P.P.” holds a special place for me. It’s a song that I resonate with on multiple levels. Like Enchanting, I value honesty and authenticity in my relationships. I’ve experienced the disappointments and struggles that come with trusting the wrong people, and this song serves as a reminder to stay true to myself.

The catchy chorus and infectious beats make “O.P.P.” an enjoyable song to vibe along to. However, it’s important to acknowledge the deeper meaning behind the lyrics. Enchanting’s storytelling and wordplay add layers of complexity to the track, elevating it from just another catchy tune to a meaningful piece of art.

Furthermore, the collaboration with Jdot Breezy and Whoppa Wit Da Choppa adds another dimension to the song. Each artist brings their unique style and perspective, further enhancing the overall experience for listeners.

Overall, “O.P.P.” by Enchanting is a song that goes beyond the surface. It explores themes of trust, loyalty, and materialism while showcasing Enchanting’s lyrical prowess. The meaning behind the lyrics resonates with many listeners, myself included, making it a standout track in her album “No Luv.”

So the next time you find yourself jamming out to “O.P.P.,” take a moment to reflect on the deeper meaning behind the lyrics. Let Enchanting’s words serve as a reminder to prioritize authenticity and honesty in your relationships.
